Adobong Pusit

Ingredients:

- 1/2 kg fresh squid (cleaned and cut into rings)

- 1/4 cup soy sauce

- 1/4 cup vinegar

- 1 head garlic (minced)

- 1 medium onion (sliced)

- 2 tablespoons cooking oil

- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

- 1/2 teaspoon sugar

- 1 bay leaf

- Salt to taste

- Water as needed
